IT-Related Ethics

Ethics in IT is crucial for responsible technology development. Professionals must follow principles that protect user privacy, data integrity, and fairness. Adhering to ethical guidelines ensures technology is developed and used in a way that respects the rights and well-being of users and society.

Examples of Ethical Guidelines

  • ACM Code of Ethics – Provides professional conduct standards for computing professionals, including honesty, fairness, and respect for privacy.
  • IEEE Code of Ethics – Emphasizes ethical behavior including public interest, integrity, and avoiding conflicts of interest.
