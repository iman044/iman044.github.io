IT-Related Ethics

Ethics in IT is crucial for responsible technology development. Professionals must follow principles that protect user privacy, data integrity, and fairness.

Examples of Ethical Guidelines

  • ACM Code of Ethics: www.acm.org/code-of-ethics
  • IEEE Code of Ethics: www.ieee.org

These guidelines stress the importance of honesty, respect, and accountability in IT-related work.
