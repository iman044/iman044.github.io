IT-Related Ethics

Ethics in IT is crucial for responsible technology development. Professionals must follow principles that protect user privacy, data integrity, and fairness. Adhering to ethical guidelines ensures that technology is developed and deployed in a manner that respects the rights of individuals and communities.

Examples of Ethical Guidelines

  • ACM Code of Ethics - This code outlines professional conduct standards for computing professionals, emphasizing honesty, fairness, and accountability.
  • IEEE Code of Ethics - The IEEE Code focuses on ethical responsibilities towards the public, client, and profession, with an emphasis on truthfulness and integrity.
